One Experience.
Infinite Memories.

The Lucky Claw is a full-size, freestanding claw machine with a vintage arcade look. Deep espresso finish, dark timber accents, warm amber lighting. It stands out at any event without trying too hard.

Prizes are chosen with each client to suit the occasion. People gather around it, come back for another go, and remember it long after the night is over.

01

Premium Cabinet

Deep espresso finish with dark timber panelling. It looks considered and sits well in any setting.

02

Illuminated Marquee

A warm cream backlit marquee up top. Classic fairground lettering that fits right in at a modern event.

03

Glass & Mirror Panels

Glass on three sides, mirrored at the back. The amber lighting inside draws people in from across the room.

04

Custom Branding Options

Mirror sticker decals and a custom front sign available. Easy ways to tie it into your event without overdoing it.
